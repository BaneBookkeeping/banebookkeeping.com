Why Bane

20+Years in Service & Trades

Before Bane Bookkeeping, co-founder Brian Conley spent over two decades in the service and trades industries — first as a technician in the field, then years in operations management, running crews and keeping jobs on schedule and on budget. That background shapes how we work: we understand job costing, equipment expenses, seasonal swings, and the paperwork trades owners actually deal with, not just textbook bookkeeping.

Bane Bookkeeping is a family-run firm based right here in Delaware County, Ohio, serving small and local businesses across central Ohio.
